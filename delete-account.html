Account Deletion Request

⚠️ Important: Deleting your account is permanent. Once processed, your profile, authentication records, and active participation metrics will be permanently wiped from our cloud database. Any shared group expenses you hosted will remain calculated anonymously to ensure your friends' ledgers don't break, but your personal identity will be entirely severed from them.

How to Request Deletion

To request the complete removal of your data, please fill out the secure form below. Deletion requests are typically processed automatically within 24 to 48 hours.

💡 Alternative In-App Deletion

If you still have the app installed on your device, you do not need to wait 24 hours for a web request. You can instantly wipe your entire data footprint by navigating to Account Hub > Delete Account directly inside the app.
