Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Data Collection

To provide deterministic math and receipt scanning, DemiTab collects basic account information (Name, Email/Phone) and image data strictly for OCR processing. Receipt images are processed by our vision engine to extract item names and prices.

2. Zero Data Selling

We do not sell your financial data, receipt data, or contact lists to third parties. We are a software utility, not a data broker. Your dining habits remain your business.

3. Cloud Processing

DemiTab utilizes secure cloud functions (via Google Firebase) to process OCR requests and maintain real-time ledgers across your friend groups. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
